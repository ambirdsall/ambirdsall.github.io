Backticks Are Fantastic

I mean, legit awesome. An example:

So, when making a symbolic link, you have to type out the absolute path to the link target. There are, I assume, good reasons for that, but having to actually do it kind of sucks. Two things help:

  1. If, as is often the case, you want to link to go in your current directory, you can totally omit the second argument
  2. Typing some shell expression inside backticks and then hitting tab expands that expression interactively

You could use the $() subshell operator with an expression and it would be logically equivalent to backticks, but backticks have one killer advantage over subshells:

  1. 33% fewer keystrokes (seriously, I hate unnecessary typing)
